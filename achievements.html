Achievements

National Bank

    In 1800, he established the Bank of France which stabilized the economy by issuing paper money that was backed by a large gold reserve.

Louisiana Purchase

    1803, Napoleon sold France's Louisiana Territy to the U.S. in an effort to gain cash to restore the French economy and military.

Conquers Europe

    Within a few years of becoming emperor in 1804, Napoleon conquered all of continental Europe including Spain which would eventually lead to Spanish colonies breaking away from Spain.

Meritocracy

    To help create an efficient and effective government, Napoleon selected officials on the basis of merit (most qualified) rather than friendship and heredity.

Education Reforms

    He created a national educational system which was focused on creating total loyalty and obedience to the Emperor.
